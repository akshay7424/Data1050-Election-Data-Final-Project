EDA Startup Success Analysis ( Author: gpu, Source: Kaggle, Dataset: ‘CAX_Startup_Data.csv’, link: https://www.kaggle.com/ajaygorkar/eda-startup-success-analysis) The author identifies several reasons why a startup succeeds: (i) founders are driven by impact, (ii) founders are committed to stick with a chosen path, (iii) startup is willing to adjust.

Startup Failure Rate ( Author: Kyril Kotashev, Source: Failory, Datasets: Global Entrepreneurship Monitor Report, 2019 Global Startup Ecosystem Ranking, Statistic Brain Research Institute etc., link: https://www.failory.com/blog/startup-failure-rate ) The author finds that 9/10 startups fail, 7.5/10 venture-backed startups fail, and 2/10 new businesses fail in the first year of operations.

What Percentage of Startups Fail? ( Author: Bobby Chernev, Source: Review 42, Dataset: x, link: https://review42.com/what-percentage-of-startups-fail/ ) Author finds that 90% of new startups fail, under 50% of businesses make it to the fifth year, only 40% of startups actually turn a profit, and the highest failure rate, at 63%, occurs in the information industry.
